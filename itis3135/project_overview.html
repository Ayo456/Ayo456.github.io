Project Overview

Overview:

Purpose:

To provide accessible information about insurance services and financial planning offered by Bimpe Onamade, a licensed insurance agent with World Financial Group. The website educates visitors about insurance products, establishes credibility through partnerships with major carriers, and facilitates client communication through contact forms and virtual meeting scheduling.

Intended Users:

Young professionals building financial foundations, growing families protecting income and planning for college, individuals preparing for retirement, seniors planning for final expenses and legacy, and anyone seeking financial literacy guidance.

Overview of Content:

Professional biography of Bimpe Onamade, detailed service descriptions (Financial Literacy, Cash Value Life Insurance, College Savings Plans, Retirement Planning, Final Expense Plans, Term Life Insurance, Annuities), partnership showcase with major carriers, professional photography, contact information, Zoom appointment scheduling, and interactive contact form.
